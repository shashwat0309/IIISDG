CLIMATE CHANGE & GLOBAL WARMING

Climate science and modeling; Impacts and adaptation assessment; Natural hazard prediction and preparedness, Impact of human interventions in climate change, Conservation Techniques, Carbon Trading, Environmental impact assessment, Audit and Biosafety, Impact on Agriculture , Fisheries, Horticulture and Floriculture.

WATER RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

Hydrologic modeling; Management of Lakes and Reservoir, Limnology, Water treatment techniques, Management of Wetlands, Aquatic diversity, Water governance, Efficiency and Innovations.

BIODIVERSITY AND ECOSYSTEM SERVICES

Listening of local flora and fauna status, threats to management , identification and conservation of biodiversity, Biological invasion, Carbon s sequestrations, Payment for ecosystem services, Aquatic and terrestrial Biodiversity Ecosystem, Animal, Plant and Microbial Biodiversity

AGRICULTURE AND LIVESTOCK

Climate resilient cropping; Modeling changing crop distribution and production; Integrated pest management; Soil, water and nutrient management; Small-scale and sustainable agro-business

GENDER AND LIVELIHOOD

Nutrition, health and well-being; Social and Environmental justice; Development for marginalized groups; Women's empowerment and enterprises in different sectors of Agriculture, Horticulture, Floriculture and Fisheries.
